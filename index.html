Hi, I am Anastasiia Chervona

My name is Anastasia, I am 31 years old and I am a fitness trainer. My job is my way of life. My main motivation is to help people unlock their potential and achieve their goals, whether it is improving their physical fitness or gaining self-confidence. I believe that through movement you can find harmony with yourself and the world.

I have a son, he is 5 years old. He inspires me to be better every day. My son is my energy, joy and the greatest achievement in life.

My hobbies are cooking, sports, traveling and hiking. Cooking for me is not only a way to feed my family, but also a creative process in which I find inspiration. Sport is my passion, and walks in the fresh air allow me to enjoy moments of unity with myself.

My dream is to live by the sea, work remotely and see as many corners of our amazing world as possible.
